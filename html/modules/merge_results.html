Merge Results

class merge_results.TwoViewAnalyzer[source]

Bases: object

Analyzes and merges vehicle detection results from two camera views.

This class processes pairs of images from different camera angles showing the same parking area, merges the detection results, and identifies various parking violations by combining information from both views.

The merged results provide a more complete and accurate representation of the parking area by eliminating duplicates and resolving conflicts between the two views.
